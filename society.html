Society, Culture & Media

Society, culture, and media are deeply connected forces that shape how people live, think, and interact. Media acts as a reflection of society while also influencing cultural growth and social behaviors.

  • Society refers to a community where people live together and share common rules, values, and goals.
  • Culture defines their traditions, art, food, clothing, and communication styles.
  • Media — like TV, newspapers, and the internet — connects all parts of society.
  • It spreads knowledge, traditions, and trends across generations and regions.
  • The balance between these three shapes our social progress and cultural identity.
