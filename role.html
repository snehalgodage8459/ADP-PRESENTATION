Role of Media in Society

  • Media acts as a bridge between the government and the public.
  • It keeps citizens updated about national and international events.
  • Media connects people from different cultures and backgrounds.
  • It promotes social awareness, education, and public debate.
  • Through digital platforms, media enables fast global communication.
  • It influences opinions, promotes democracy, and supports transparency.
